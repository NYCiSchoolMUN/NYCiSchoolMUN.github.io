International Co-Prosperity Committee

iCPC:


The iCPC committee occurs after the events of The Handmaid’s Tale, wherein Gilead is slowly crumbling. The Eyes and Mayday have fallen into splinter groups, so that tension between Gilead within itself and between other countries has only increased. Chaos ensues. World and rebel leaders gather here today to discuss solutions, aiming to, above all, finally promote a sense of world peace.

iDRUG

International Drug Resistance Untimely Guerilla United

iDRUG:

It is June of 2019 and there is a growing war on drugs throughout many Latin American countries triggering massive instability. With new acts of violence from gangs, cartels, and drugs lords throughout this region, delegates must act quickly to stop this war from spreading on further. This committee integrates real-life crisis of the drug war with a fantasy twist of events.

Chair(s): Gio Torres-Lorenzotti & André Eisenberg
